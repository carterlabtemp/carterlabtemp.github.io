# Open positions

We are always looking for new group members with passion, talent, and motivation! 

#### PhD and Postdoc positions
If you are interested in working with us as a PhD student or postdoc, please send me an [email](mailto:hkcarter@ucsd.edu). State briefly why you are interested and attach a CV. **Important**: please insert _"Application PhD"_ or _"Application Postdoc"_ in the subject line.

#### Masters students
If you are a Masters student at UC San Diego looking for a Masters project, contact me via email or stop by my office. 

#### Undergraduate Students
If you are interested in working with us as an undergrad, and have coding experience (Python, R), send me an [email](mailto:hkcarter@ucsd.edu)
